Advocacy

As a Computer Science student, I am passionate about harnessing the power of technology to solve real-world problems and create a more equitable and inclusive society. I believe that technology should not only be a tool for innovation but also a force for positive social change. In an era where technology is rapidly evolving, I advocate for its ethical development and responsible use. From artificial intelligence to data privacy, I am committed to promoting practices that prioritize transparency, fairness, and inclusivity. My goal is to ensure that technological advancements benefit everyone, particularly those from marginalized communities who are often left behind in the digital divide. I strongly believe that access to technology is a fundamental right, and I work towards closing the gap by supporting initiatives that provide digital literacy, resources, and opportunities to underserved populations. Additionally, I am committed to advancing ethical frameworks in technology that address issues such as algorithmic bias, accessibility, and cybersecurity.
