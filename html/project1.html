Cleft2Speech

A web application speech correction system for cleft patients.

About the Project

The Cleft2Speech is a web-based application designed to assist patients recovering from cleft repair surgery in improving their articulation and speech quality. The system leverages advanced technologies such as Transformer-based Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), Phoneme Mapping, and Speech Synthesis to create an immersive, patient-centric experience aimed at improving speech patterns and restoring vocal confidence.

This application aims to bridge the gap between medical recovery and speech rehabilitation by enabling patients to practice and correct their speech in a supportive and intuitive digital environment.

Key Features

Transformer-based ASR Engine

The system can process and transcribe real-time speech, making it suitable for dynamic correction and feedback.

Phoneme Mapping and Error Detection

The system utilizes phoneme mapping to compare the patient’s speech with predefined correct phonetic and graphemic models.

Speech Synthesis with Voice Cloning

Patients can listen to their own corrected speech, which provides a clearer model for them to emulate in subsequent practice sessions.

Intuitive Web-Based Interface

Patients can simply speak into the microphone to receive real-time feedback and listen to corrected speech, creating an engaging and non-intimidating rehabilitation experience.
